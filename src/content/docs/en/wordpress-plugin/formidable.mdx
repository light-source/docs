---
title: 'Formidable'
description: 'Formidable support.'
---

The Procaptcha plugin extends [Formidable](https://wordpress.org/plugins/formidable/) by registering the `Prosopo Procaptcha` field, which you can easily add to your forms for Procaptcha protection.

To enable this, open the target form and insert the `Prosopo Procaptcha` field anywhere in the form, above the `Submit` button.

* [How to add CAPTCHA to Formidable](https://prosopo.io/wordpress/how-to-add-captcha-to-formidable-forms/)
